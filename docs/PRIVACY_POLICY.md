**Don't Die** ("the app") helps you avoid distracted driving. We collect the
minimum needed to do that.

## What we collect

- **Device identifier and token** — a random ID created when the app first
  runs, used to associate your drive sessions and rewards with your device.
  It is not your Apple ID or advertising ID.
- **Phone number** — only if you link it, so calls forwarded while you drive
  can be matched to your account.
- **Drive session times** — when drive mode starts and ends, to compute your
  data reward.
- **Missed call metadata** — the caller's number and time of calls received
  while you were driving, so we can show them to you afterward. We never
  access call audio or message content beyond an inbound text that triggers
  the automatic "I'm driving" reply.
- **Location and motion data** — processed **on your device only** to detect
  driving. Your location is never sent to our servers.

## What we don't do

- We do not sell or share your data with advertisers or data brokers.
- We do not track your location history.
- We do not read your contacts, messages, or call history on the phone.
